About HP Gas

HP Gas is a leading provider of LPG (liquefied petroleum gas) in India, catering to millions of households across the country. Established with a commitment to safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction, HP Gas has been a trusted name for over 40 years.

Our mission is to deliver clean, safe, and efficient energy solutions to every household, ensuring convenience and peace of mind for our customers.

Our Vision

To be the preferred choice for LPG in every household, driven by innovation, sustainability, and excellence in service.

Our Values

  • Customer-centric approach
  • Integrity and transparency
  • Continuous improvement
  • Sustainability
